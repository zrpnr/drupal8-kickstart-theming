Responsive Images

  • Supports <img> & <picture>
  • Includes picturefill polyfill
  • Includes Breakpoint module
Responsive images doesn't just mean scaling to fit your screen. If you view a page on a small screen it doesn't make sense to download the same size image that would look good on a wide studio display. The picture element is supported in most browsers, but D8 includes the picturefill polyfill for browsers that don't. *cough IE The breakpoint modules lets you define widths to choose different image styles, which prints out the picture with the srcsets and fallback filled in.
